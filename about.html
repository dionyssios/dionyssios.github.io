About me

Hello I am Dionysis a M.Sc. mathematician from Greece, working over 12 years as a tutor, find out more about me in my curriculum vitae. For my online teaching I use Bitpaper a whiteboard that allows both tutor and student write at the same time which resemble the face-to-face tutoring to a great extend. Also I use Geogebra (software) which can present difficult mathematical notions dynamically in an interactive way. As regards the process of teaching I have attached on the half part of the whiteboard my personal notes and on the other half I solve similar tasks interactively with the student . To get a better feeling about my M.O (modus operandi) feel free to book a half hour demonstration (link) and if you are happy then we can discuss the fees and the schedule. Online lessons tend to be better than face-to-face because you can record the lesson and keep track of any second so you can come back anytime to understand any obscure point.
